TOURIST PLACE

Dr.Soetomo’s Monument

Dr.Soetomo was born in Nganjuk and this monument was built to respect him as a national hero. The place that used to be a monument at the Ngepeh village was his grandmother’s house.

Roro Kuning Waterfall

Placed in 600 metres above sea level with height 10-15 metres. This waterfall is under Wilis Mountain. In there, we also can found Jenderal Soedirman’s Monument, that built to respect Jenderal Soedirman.

Ngetos Temple

This temple placed in Ngetos village, Ngetos district. Based on its shape this temple was made in the 15 century, during Majapahit kingdom. The expert’s estimate says that it was a Hayam Wuruk’s funeral place.
