NGANJUK REGENCY

Nganjuk Regency is a regency in East Java, Indonesia also known as “Kota Angin” or “Wind City”.

It bordered Bojonegoro Regency in the north, Jombang Regency in the east, Kediri Regency in the south, and Madiun Regency in the west.

It had a population of 1,045,598 at the 2014 census. Nganjuk regency is divided into 20 districs.
