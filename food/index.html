Nasi Becek

Nasi becek or sego becek is similar to Soto but it uses goat meat and onion slices. Usually nasi becek eaten together with sate kambing/goat satay.

Dumbleg

Dumbleg was made from brown sugar, flour and coconut milk. It has a sweet taste and is wrapped in pelepah pinang.
